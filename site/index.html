( About Us )

THE
GOSPEL
FOR ALL
NATIONS

All Nations Christian Fellowship Church is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to every nation, tribe, and tongue.

Founded on the principles of faith, love, and unity, ANCFC is a place where people from all walks of life come together to worship, grow, and serve. Under the anointed leadership of Apostle Dr. Moses Mukwiza and Apostle Nathalie Mukwiza, our church has become a beacon of hope and transformation in the community.

We believe in the power of the Word of God to change lives, restore families, and build nations. Our mission is to equip believers for the work of ministry and to be a light in the darkness.

01

Our Vision

To reach all nations with the transforming power of the Gospel, raising a generation of kingdom-minded believers who impact their communities and the world.

02

Our Mission

To worship God in Spirit and truth, disciple believers into spiritual maturity, and serve our communities through love, compassion, and the demonstration of God's power.

03

Our Belief

We believe in one God manifested in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. We hold firm to the authority of the Bible as the infallible Word of God.

( Our Leadership )

ANOINTED
LEADERSHIP

( Senior Pastor & Founder )

Apostle Dr.
Moses Mukwiza

Apostle Dr. Moses Mukwiza is a man called and anointed by God to lead nations into the fullness of their divine destiny. With a heart burning for souls and a prophetic mandate to preach the uncompromised Word of God, he has dedicated his life to building the Kingdom of God across nations.

As the founder and senior pastor of All Nations Christian Fellowship Church, Apostle Moses carries a powerful apostolic and prophetic anointing that has transformed countless lives through healing, deliverance, and the demonstration of God's power.

( Co-Pastor )

Apostle
Nathalie Mukwiza

Apostle Nathalie Mukwiza serves faithfully alongside her husband as co-pastor of All Nations Christian Fellowship Church. She is a woman of prayer, grace, and deep spiritual insight whose ministry has been a source of strength and encouragement to women, families, and the entire congregation.

Her passion for nurturing believers and her gift of hospitality make ANCFC a warm and welcoming home for all who walk through our doors. Together with Apostle Moses, she leads with a servant's heart and a vision for kingdom impact.

“Our hearts beat for the nations. We are called to bring the light of Christ to every corner of the earth.”

— Apostle Dr. Moses & Apostle Nathalie Mukwiza
